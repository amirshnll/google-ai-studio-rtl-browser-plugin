# Privacy Policy

## Overview
This browser extension is designed to enhance the user experience on Google AI Studio by providing Right-to-Left (RTL) support and font customization options.

## Data Collection and Use
We are committed to protecting your privacy. This extension **does not collect, store, share, or transmit** any personal information, browsing history, or user data to any external servers. All functionality is processed locally on your machine.

## Local Storage
We use the browser's `chrome.storage.local` API exclusively to save your personal preferences, such as:
- RTL toggles
- Selected font configurations
- Custom CSS selectors

This information remains strictly within your browser and is never uploaded or shared.

## Permissions Explanation
To provide its functionality, this extension requires the following permissions:
- **`storage`**: Used to save and retrieve your custom settings locally.
- **`scripting` & `activeTab`**: Used to inject styling changes into the current webpage when you interact with the extension.
- **`fontSettings`**: Used to query your system's available fonts to allow you to select your preferred typeface.
